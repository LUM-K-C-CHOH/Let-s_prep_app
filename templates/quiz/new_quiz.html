Pro tip

Cleaner notes → better questions

LET'S PREP works best when your upload is focused and well-structured. A few tweaks to your notes can dramatically improve the quality of the questions.

  • ✔
    Use headings & bullet points.
    Break your notes into sections like concepts, formulas, and examples.
  • ✔
    Keep one topic per file.

  • ✔
    Highlight key definitions & “why it matters”.
    Those become perfect flashcards and conceptual questions.
